5 in One
Rules:
     •Each team should consist of 5 participants
      (2 girls compulsory).

     •The left hand of the left side last person and
      right hand of the right side last person must be free.

     •Task will be assigned one after another (as soon
       as team finishes a particular task, next task
       will be assigned). If the team fails to perform
       the task then they will be disqualified from the event.

     •Team which finishes all the tasks first,
       will win the contest.
